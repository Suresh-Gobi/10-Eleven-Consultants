What are the benefits of setting up a Free Zone company?

Free Zones offer 100% foreign ownership, tax exemptions, and streamlined processes for international businesses. Some zones are industry-specific, which can provide additional benefits tailored to your business activity.

Can a Free Zone company trade directly with UAE customers?

Yes, free zone companies can trade directly with UAE residents and businesses without restrictions, providing greater market access.

How long does it take to set up a company in the UAE?

The timeline varies based on the jurisdiction you choose. Free Zone setups or Mainland companies for specific activities can take as little as 3–5 working days.

What documents are required for company formation?

Common requirements include passport copies of shareholders, proof of address, business plan, and initial approval from relevant authorities. We guide you through the full list based on your specific setup.
