About Us

Introduction To The Best
Business Consultants

With 15+ years of experience in the industry, 10Eleven Consultants is committed to helping entrepreneurs, startups, and international businesses achieve their goals by offering comprehensive business formation solutions in mainland, free zones, and offshore jurisdictions. We are dedicated to assisting entrepreneurs and businesses in seamlessly navigating the complex process of starting and running a business in the UAE. From selecting the ideal business structure to managing technical, administrative, and financial requirements, we provide end-to-end support. Our expertise lies in offering tailored business establishment services for individual entrepreneurs and corporate entities. Our team of experienced professionals provides expert guidance in choosing the right corporate structure and jurisdiction, ensuring every detail of the setup process is meticulously managed. At 10Eleven Consultants, we pride ourselves on being more than just consultants—we are a trusted partner, offering complete support and serving as a reliable guide through the intricacies of UAE business practices. Our expertise ensures that clients are well-equipped to navigate the often unfamiliar terrain of regulations, opportunities, and challenges. As a bridge between global businesses and the UAE market, we connect international entities to this dynamic region, helping them unlock new opportunities and expand their horizons. We recognize that establishing your business is only the first step. That’s why our services extend beyond company formation. We assist with critical post-setup operations such as accounting, bank account opening, and other essential business functions, ensuring your company succeeds and thrives in the competitive UAE market.

Our Mission

Our mission is to add our core values to build the basement and empower the vision of every entrepreneurs, startups and businesses. We as a team, simplify the process of setting up or expansion within the one of world's most dynamic business hub.
